Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our roofing services

Common signs include missing or damaged shingles, water stains on ceilings, leaks during rain, sagging areas, granules in gutters, and visible cracks or damage. If you notice any of these signs, contact us for a free inspection. We recommend annual roof inspections to catch problems early before they become costly repairs.

Minor repairs typically take 1-2 days, depending on the extent of damage and weather conditions. More extensive repairs may take longer. Emergency repairs can often be completed within 24 hours. We provide a detailed timeline estimate during our inspection and work efficiently to minimize disruption to your home.

Yes! We offer free roof inspections for homeowners in the Portland area. Our certified technicians will thoroughly examine your roof, identify any issues, and provide a detailed report with photos. We'll also give you a transparent, no-obligation quote for any recommended repairs or services.

We work with all major roofing materials including asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, slate, wood shakes, and flat roofing systems. Each material has its own benefits, and we can help you choose the best option for your home based on your budget, aesthetic preferences, and the climate in Portland.

Absolutely! We offer 24/7 emergency roofing services for urgent situations such as storm damage, severe leaks, or structural issues. Roof repair costs vary depending on the extent of damage, materials needed, and labor required. Basic inspections start at $99, minor repairs from $199, leak repairs from $249, and emergency repairs from $299. Full roof replacements start at $3,500. We provide detailed, transparent quotes before starting any work, with no hidden fees.

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured. All our roofing contractors hold the proper licenses required by Oregon state law, and we carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. This protects both you and our team members during the roofing project.

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. We offer workmanship warranties on all our installations and repairs, and manufacturer warranties on materials. The specific warranty terms depend on the service and materials used. We'll provide full warranty details with your quote.

The lifespan of a roof depends on the materials used and maintenance. Asphalt shingles typically last 20-30 years, metal roofs 40-70 years, tile roofs 50+ years, and slate roofs can last over 100 years. Regular maintenance and prompt repairs can significantly extend your roof's lifespan.

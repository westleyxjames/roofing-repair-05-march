PREVENTIVE CARE

Professional Roof Inspection

Regular roof inspections are the key to catching small problems before they become expensive disasters. Our certified inspectors provide comprehensive evaluations with detailed reports and recommendations.

Inspection Includes:

  • Surface Inspection - Check all shingles, tiles, or metal panels
  • Structural Assessment - Evaluate decking and support structures
  • Flashing & Seals - Inspect all penetrations and joints
  • Drainage System - Check gutters, downspouts, and water flow
  • Interior Check - Look for signs of leaks or damage inside
  • Photo Documentation - Detailed images of all findings
  • Written Report - Comprehensive findings and recommendations

💰 FREE for Portland residents

No obligation • Completed in 1-2 hours

URGENT REPAIR

Roof Leak Detection & Repair

A leaking roof can cause thousands of dollars in water damage. Our leak detection specialists use advanced technology to find and fix leaks quickly, preventing further damage to your home.

Common Leak Causes:

  • Damaged or missing shingles allowing water penetration
  • Cracked or deteriorated flashing around chimneys and vents
  • Clogged gutters causing water backup
  • Ice dam formation in winter months
  • Skylights with failed seals

💰 Starting at $249

Same-day service available
